INTRODUCTION

Ucore_Plus was used for OS Experiments & Research in OS Course Of Dept. of Computer Science & Technology, Tsinghua University.

ucore_plus is a nano OS which is derived from xv6&jos in MIT, OS161 in Harvard and Linux. ucore was developed and used in Department of Computer Science & Technology, Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University. The codes in the files that constitute xv6&jos are Copyright (2006-Current) Frans Kaashoek, Robert Morris, and Russ Cox and uses MIT License. The codes in the files that constitute OS/161 are written by David A. Holland.
